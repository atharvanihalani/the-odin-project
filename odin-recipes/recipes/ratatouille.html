Ratatouille

Description

Indulge in this classic Ratatouille, a vibrant and flavorful dish that'll transport you straight to the heart of France. Packed with fresh veggies and herbs, it's a healthy and delicious way to enjoy the best of summer produce.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 2 yellow squashes
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 2 zucchinis

Steps

  1. Using a sharp knife, slice the eggplants, tomatoes, squash, and zucchini into thin rounds.
  2. Make the sauce: Heat the olive oil on medium. Saut√© the onion, garlic, and bell peppers. Season, and add the tomatoes. Stir, and smooth the sauce.
  3. Arrange the veggies in alternating slices on top of the sauce, working from the outer edge to the center of the pan.
