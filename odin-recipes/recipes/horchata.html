Horchata

Description

Horchata: A popular & refreshing Mexican drink made with ground rice, milk and cinnamon!!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 2 cinammon sticks
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Place the rice, cinnamon stick and 4 cups of water into a bowl. Cover the bowl and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Add this mixture to a blender. Puree for about 4 minutes.
  3. Strain this mixture over a pitcher.
  4. Cover the pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover, then bake for another 20 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.
  5. Pour in the milks, vanilla, and 4 cups of water.
  6. Chill and stir before serving over ice.
